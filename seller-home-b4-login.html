Sell your tickets on Slicket

STEP 1

After you've found your Event, Enter Ticket Details

STEP 2

Decide if you want to Sell Now (for the available Bids in the system)

STEP 3

If not, List your tickets (and wait for buyer to buy)

STEP 4

Negotiate your with any Buyer (in the Marketplace)

FAQ

What is a listing?

A listing is the total number of tickets I have for sale in a row, that are next to each other, in a section.

How do I Sell Now?

Upon entering your listing, bids that match your ticket details are displayed enabling you to select any bid(s) and sell now. Additionally, after your tickets have been listed, go to the "My Ticket Listings" or "Event" page and select any of your listings. By highlighting your listing you will be able to view all the bids that match your tickets. Select any bid(s) and follow the checkout process.

How do I Negotiate?

After your tickets have been listed you are encouraged to negotiate with any bidder that has published a bid. Additionally, any user may send a negotiation to you and you may accept, counter negotiate or decline.

Can I list my ticket as an Instant Download?

Upon entering your listing you will be asked your ticket type and delivery method. Your e-tickets may then be uploaded and displayed as an Instant Download for buyers.

Additionally, if you prefer to upload after your tickets have been listed, go to the "My Ticket Listings" and select any of your listings. By highlighting your listing you can then upload your tickets so they can be displayed as an Instant Download to buyers.

Must I sell my tickets to a bidder or negotiator?

Absolutely not! We desire that you Stake Your Position.

We created the only fair and efficient market for buyers and sellers to come together and negotiate a fair price. If you don't like any buyers price you are not obligated to sell. You are, however, obligated to sell at your listing price
